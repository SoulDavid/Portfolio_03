CAN'T TOUCH THIS

2021

Can't Touch This is an arcade-style game developed for the PC in which the player will have to shoot the red cubes to increase the high score and will have to dodge the green ones.

During the development of the project, I took care of basic aspects of the player: reload, score and death, as well as the HUD and particles.
