NECROCANDOR

2020 - 2021

Step into the shoes of the minions of a master of evil. Collaborate, compete or betray in this two-player local multiplayer platformer. Enter the enchanted mansion of Palesia and avoid its innumerable traps to get the relic that it protects in its entrails.

In charge of programming mechanics such as the hook or the impulse, and of putting lighting and shaders (Universal Render Pipeline), according to what is desired in the design department.
