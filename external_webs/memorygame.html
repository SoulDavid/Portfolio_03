MEMORY GAME MANGA'S VERSION

2021

Typical card game that consisted of revealing the pairs. The cards are distributed in such a way that you don't know where they are and you have to remember which is which.

It all started as a project that was sent to us as an exercise in a programming exam. I liked the idea, and after a while, I went back to do it in more detail in terms of mechanics.
